Privacy Policy for Autofill Factorial

Last Updated: [Date]

1. Information Collection

Autofill Factorial does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The permissions requested by the extension are solely for its functionality and include:

  • Active Tab Permission: To access the currently active tab for executing its features locally.
  • Storage Permission: To save user preferences and schedules directly on the user's device.
  • Host Permissions: To interact with specific websites required for the extension's features.

No data is sent to any external server or third party.

2. Data Usage

Any data stored by Autofill Factorial is kept locally on the user's device. It is not accessible by anyone other than the user.

3. Third-Party Services

Autofill Factorial does not use any third-party services or external analytics tools.

4. Security

Autofill Factorial does not transmit any data, ensuring user privacy and security. All functionality operates entirely within the user's browser.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
